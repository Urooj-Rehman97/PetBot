Got Questions? we've got answers

  • What exactly is a PET bottle?

    Great question! PET stands for polyethylene terephthalate. It’s a super strong, lightweight plastic that's perfect for packaging everything from your favorite beverages to cosmetics and even household products. It’s crystal-clear, durable, and one of the most recyclable plastics out there. In short? It’s a trusty companion for all your liquid needs

  • Are PET bottles safe for food and drink?

    Absolutely! Our PET bottles are designed with food-grade standards in mind, so they’re totally safe for storing your beverages and other consumables. No BPA, no worries. Whether it's a chilled soda or refreshing juice, your drink is in good hands.

  • Can I recycle my PET bottles?

    You bet! PET bottles are 100% recyclable. In fact, PET is one of the most widely recycled plastics globally. Once you're done with that bottle, make sure to give it a quick rinse and pop it into the recycling bin. Together, we can keep the planet happy and healthy

  • Are our bottles eco-friendly?

    Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. We use recycled PET (rPET) wherever possible to reduce waste and give plastic a second life. Plus, all of our bottles are 100% recyclable, so they’re good for the environment—good for you, and good for Mother Earth

  • Can I reuse my PET bottle?

    While PET bottles are great for single use, if you choose to reuse them, just make sure they’re cleaned thoroughly. For long-term reuse, however, we recommend using bottles specifically designed for multiple uses (like reusable water bottles). PET bottles are made for convenience, but if you're looking to be extra eco-conscious, recycling them is the way to go

  • Can PET bottles hold hot liquids?

    PET bottles are perfect for cold and room-temperature liquids, but they’re not the best for hot stuff. If you want to store a hot beverage, like coffee or tea, we recommend using containers made for high temperatures to keep everything safe and secure.

  • Do your bottles come in custom sizes or designs?

    Yes, yes, and yes! Whether you're a business looking to create branded bottles or you need a custom size for a special project, we’ve got options. Just drop us a line, and we’ll help you create the perfect bottle for your needs.

  • How long do PET bottles last?

    PET bottles have a long shelf life when stored properly. Keep them in a cool, dry place, away from sunlight, and they’ll stay in tip-top shape. If you’re using the bottles to store liquids, be sure to check the expiration date on the contents to be safe

  • How do I get in touch with customer support?

    Got more questions? We’re here for you! You can contact us by email at [support@petbottlescompany.com], call us at [phone number], or chat with us directly on our website. We love hearing from you

  • Are your PET bottles UV-resistant?

    Yes! PET bottles offer some natural resistance to UV light, which helps protect your beverages or liquids from harmful sunlight exposure. But for the best shelf life and taste, we still recommend storing bottles in a cool, dark place whenever possible.

  • Can I store carbonated drinks in PET bottles?

    Absolutely! PET bottles are perfect for carbonated beverages, thanks to their strength and flexibility. They can handle the pressure from carbonated drinks like soda, sparkling water, and kombucha. Just make sure the bottles are properly sealed to avoid any fizzing out
